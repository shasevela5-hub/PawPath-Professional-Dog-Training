# New Training Content Design for PawPath

This document outlines the design for two new premium training paths: **Advanced Obedience** and **Trick Training**, along with their respective exercises.

## 1. Advanced Obedience (Premium Path)

**Path ID:** `advanced-obedience`
**Title:** Advanced Obedience
**Category:** `advanced-obedience` (new category)
**Description:** Elevate your dog's obedience skills with advanced commands, off-leash control, and reliable performance in distracting environments.
**Is Premium:** True
**Life Stages:** Adolescent, Adult
**Difficulty:** Advanced
**Estimated Weeks:** 10
**Icon:** `command.circle.fill` (new icon)

### Exercises:

## 2. Trick Training (Premium Path)

**Path ID:** `trick-training`
**Title:** Trick Training
**Category:** `trick-training` (new category)
**Description:** Unleash your dog's inner performer with fun and impressive tricks that strengthen your bond. 
**Is Premium:** True
**Life Stages:** Puppy, Adolescent, Adult
**Difficulty:** Intermediate
**Estimated Weeks:** 8
**Icon:** `magic.wand.and.stars` (new icon)

### Exercises:

| ID | Title | Description | Difficulty | Estimated Minutes | Week | Video URL | Steps | Tips | Required Equipment |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `trick-1` | **Shake a Paw** | Teach your dog to offer their paw for a handshake. | Beginner | 10 | 1 | | 1. Ask your dog to sit. 2. Gently tap their paw and say "Shake". 3. When they lift their paw, take it and reward. 4. Repeat until they offer paw on command. | Be gentle; use a clear verbal cue; practice both paws; make it fun. | Treats |
| `trick-2` | **High Five** | Teach your dog to give a high five. | Intermediate | 10 | 1 | | 1. Start with "Shake a Paw". 2. Hold your hand higher, encouraging them to reach up. 3. Say "High Five" and reward when they touch your hand. 4. Gradually increase height. | Build on "Shake"; use an open palm; reward for touching your hand, not just lifting paw. | Treats |
| `trick-3` | **Spin/Twirl** | Teach your dog to spin in a circle on command. | Intermediate | 15 | 2 | | 1. Hold a treat near your dog's nose. 2. Lure them in a circle, saying "Spin" or "Twirl". 3. Reward when they complete the circle. 4. Gradually fade the lure. | Start in a small circle; practice both directions; keep it fast and fun; use a clear verbal cue. | Treats |
| `trick-4` | **Play Dead (Bang!)** | Teach your dog to lie down and roll over on their side. | Advanced | 20 | 2 | | 1. Ask your dog to "Down". 2. Lure them onto their side with a treat, saying "Bang!". 3. Reward when they are on their side. 4. Gradually remove the lure. | Build on "Down"; be patient; make it dramatic and fun; use a hand signal. | Treats |
| `trick-5` | **Bow** | Teach your dog to bow, with front paws down and rear up. | Intermediate | 15 | 3 | | 1. Hold a treat near your dog's chest, then move it between their front paws. 2. As they lower their front, say "Bow". 3. Reward when they are in the bow position. 4. Practice holding the position. | Lure slowly; reward for correct body posture; practice after stretching. | Treats |
| `trick-6` | **Weave Through Legs** | Teach your dog to weave through your legs as you walk. | Advanced | 20 | 3 | | 1. Stand with legs apart. 2. Lure your dog through your legs with a treat. 3. As they go through, step forward with the opposite leg. 4. Add verbal cue "Weave". | Start slowly; use a continuous lure; reward for each successful pass; build rhythm. | Treats |
| `trick-7` | **Fetch Leash** | Teach your dog to retrieve their leash. | Intermediate | 15 | 4 | | 1. Place the leash nearby. 2. Encourage your dog to pick it up. 3. Say "Fetch Leash" and reward when they bring it to you. 4. Gradually increase distance. | Make it a positive experience; use a lightweight leash; reward for bringing it all the way. | Leash, Treats |
| `trick-8` | **Clean Up (Put Toys Away)** | Teach your dog to put their toys into a basket. | Advanced | 25 | 4 | | 1. Teach "Take it" and "Drop it" first. 2. Place a toy near the basket. 3. Lure your dog to pick up the toy and drop it in the basket. 4. Add "Clean Up" command. | Start with one toy; use a wide-mouthed basket; reward for each toy placed. | Toys, Toy basket, Treats |
